What is Wi-fi Calling?
Wi-Fi calling bridges the gap when cellular signals falter, allowing you to make calls over a Wi-Fi network. This feature ensures your connectivity is constant, crisp, and clear, using any available Wi-Fi connection to enhance your call quality and reach.

Why Use Wi-fi Calling?
Enjoy the freedom of making calls without a cellular signal, reduce your data usage, and potentially save on international charges. It's perfect for when you're indoors or in areas with poor cell reception.
Process
How Wi-fi Calling Works
Simply connect your phone to Wi-Fi, go to your phone's settings to enable Wi-Fi calling, and make your calls as usual. Your phone automatically handles the switch, ensuring your communication is seamless.
